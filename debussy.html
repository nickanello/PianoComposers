Claude Debussy

Born in 1862, Debussy was a French composer who was known for his unique and innovative compositions. Many people regard him as a pioneer of impressionism in music composition, which is a style that focuses on conveying the emotions elicited by some subject. His pieces often switch keys, or do not fully adhere to the key they are written in, which creates a unique style of composition. Debussy died of cancer at the age of 55.
