This site gives the user information about three classical piano composers. It provides them with their names, images, a short bio, and a few pieces of music that they have composed. This site can be expanded by adding more composers, elaborating on each bio, and adding more compositions to the tables. Also, this website could be a subset of a larger site that gives information about composers of instruments other than piano as well.
